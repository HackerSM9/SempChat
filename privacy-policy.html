Privacy Policy for SempChat

Last Updated: November 1, 2024

1. Information Collection

SempChat does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All chat sessions and authentication information (username and PIN) are only held temporarily during each active chat session and are deleted automatically after the session ends.

2. Data Usage

We only use your username and PIN to authenticate and create a secure connection between you and your selected chat partner. No data is retained after the session ends.

3. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS protocols to encrypt data transmitted between users, protecting your connection from unauthorized access. Additionally, SempChat does not save or retain any user data, chat messages, or PINs.

4. Third-Party Links

SempChat does not use any third-party tracking or analytic services. We also do not display ads or include links to external sites.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage users to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect their privacy.
